Enrollment and Eligibility:

1. Learners must be of legal driving age and meet all eligibility requirements set by local authorities. 2. The learner must provide valid identification and necessary documentation for enrollment.

3. All fees must be paid in full before the commencement of any driving lessons. 4. Payments are non-refundable, except under exceptional circumstances as determined by the driving school.

Scheduling and Cancellations:

5. Lessons must be scheduled in advance, and changes require a minimum notice period. 6. Cancellations made without sufficient notice may result in a fee.

Driving Lesson Conduct:

7. Learners must arrive on time for scheduled lessons; lateness may result in a shortened session. 8. The learner is expected to be in a suitable physical and mental condition for each lesson. 9. Any use of alcohol or drugs before or during lessons is strictly prohibited.

10. Learners are responsible for treating the training vehicle with care and reporting any issues promptly.

License Examination:
