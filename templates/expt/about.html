About Us

Expert Yomo Driving School

Welcome to Expert Yomo Driving School, a distinguished institution with a rich legacy of over 15 years in the driving industry in Kenya. Under the visionary leadership of Sir Yuvenalis Ongaki Magare, who has served as the Director for the past 15 years, we have consistently set the standard for excellence in driving education.

At Expert Yomo Driving School, we take pride in our unwavering commitment to educating the Kenyan community on best road practices. Our mission is not only to produce skilled drivers but also to instill a deep sense of responsibility and adherence to the traffic act among both future and existing drivers.

With our continuous growth and expansion, Expert Yomo Driving School has established branches across Kenya, including prominent locations such as Nairobi, Kisii, Keoka, Nyamache, Kebirigo, and more. This strategic presence allows us to reach and serve a diverse audience, contributing to safer roads nationwide.

We are renowned for our transparency in all our dealings and take pride in offering a wide range of vehicle categories and packages. Our comprehensive approach ensures that our students receive tailored education to meet their specific needs. From small cars to heavy vehicles, we cover it all.

Over the years, Expert Yomo Driving School has successfully educated over half a million drivers. These individuals proudly relate to our school as the source of their comprehensive driving education. Our success is a testament to the quality of our programs and the dedication of our experienced instructors.

At Expert Yomo Driving School, we don't just teach people how to drive; we empower them with the knowledge and skills to navigate the roads safely. Join us on the journey towards responsible and skilled driving. Your safety is our priority.
